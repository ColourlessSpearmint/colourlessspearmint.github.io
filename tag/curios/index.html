Curios

Curio (noun) - a curious and intriguing object of collection

Posts tagged as Curios are short essays about interesting things I've found. They're similar to a TIL (Today I Learned), in that I'm just bringing something I found to the reader's attention. There's a fuzzy line between a Curio post and an in-depth analysis post, but the difference is usually in the amount of effort I put into it. If it was more effort to find the topic than to write the post, it's a Curio.
