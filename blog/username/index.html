My Username

June 7, 2025

This site's URL is "colourlessspearmint.github.io". The ".github.io" part is because it's hosted via GitHub Pages, and the "colourlessspearmint" comes from my username, ColourlessSpearmint.

My username is primarily a reference to a novel called There Is No Antimemetics Division by Sam Hughes, one of my favorite authors. One of the chapters is titled "CASE COLOURLESS GREEN". I thought that sounded interesting and oxymoronic, so I decided to shamelessly steal it.

The "spearmint" references from my favorite colour, Spearmint Teal.

I came up with it on a whim on September 26, 2024, when I was trying to think up a semi-quasi-sort-of-professional-sounding (i.e. not named after my favorite animal) username for my Github account. I've stuck with it out of inertia and to build a consistent online presence.
